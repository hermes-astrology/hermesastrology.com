2. Taurus Personality Traits

Taurus individuals are steady, loyal, and deeply grounded in the physical world. They are here to build, preserve, and enjoy the fruits of life.

Core Taurus Energy: Taurus represents the principle of “I HAVE.” Through patience and consistency, Taurus manifests lasting value.

Reliable

Dependable and trustworthy in all areas of life.

Sensual

Strong appreciation for touch, taste, beauty, and comfort.

Patient

Willing to move slowly for long-term success.

Stubborn

Once decided, difficult to sway.

3. Symbolism & Mythology

The Sacred Bull

Taurus is linked to ancient bull symbolism found in Minoan, Egyptian, and Greek cultures. The bull represents fertility, wealth, and the generative power of the Earth.

  • The Bull: strength, fertility, endurance
  • Venus: love, beauty, value
  • Earth: stability and manifestation
