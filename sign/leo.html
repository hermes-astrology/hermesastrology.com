2. Leo Personality Traits

Leo individuals are expressive, confident, and deeply motivated by purpose and love. Their mission is to radiate their authentic self and inspire others through example.

Core Leo Energy: Leo represents the principle of “I CREATE.” Through joy, passion, and courage, Leo learns to lead from the heart.

Charismatic

Naturally magnetic, commanding attention without effort.

Creative

Strong drive to express identity through art, leadership, or performance.

Loyal

Deeply protective and devoted to loved ones.

Proud

Needs respect and recognition to feel fully alive.

3. Symbolism & Mythology

The Nemean Lion

Leo is linked to the Nemean Lion slain by Hercules as his first labor — a symbol of courage, strength, and immortal glory.

  • The Sun: identity, life force, kingship
  • The Lion: bravery, authority, heart-centered leadership
  • Gold: divine radiance and creative fire
