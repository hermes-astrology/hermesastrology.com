2. Libra Personality Traits

Core Libra Energy: Libra represents “WE ARE.” It is the sign where self-awareness matures into relational awareness, diplomacy, and ethical balance.

Diplomatic

Natural mediators who seek fairness.

Charming

Graceful, socially intelligent, refined.

Romantic

Values emotional and aesthetic harmony.

Idealistic

Believes in justice and mutual respect.

3. Symbolism & Mythology

Libra is linked to Astraea, goddess of justice, whose scales weigh human actions. Libra symbolizes moral order, law, and the balance between extremes.
