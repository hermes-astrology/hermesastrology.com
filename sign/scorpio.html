3. Symbolism & Mythology

The Underworld Initiate

Scorpio is associated with Hades (Pluto), ruler of the underworld. The sign symbolizes descent into darkness for the sake of transformation.

  • Greek: Pluto / Hades
  • Egyptian: Osiris (death and resurrection)
  • Alchemy: Nigredo (blackening phase)
