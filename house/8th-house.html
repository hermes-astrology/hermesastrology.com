The 8th House in Astrology

Transformation, Intimacy & the Alchemy of Power

The House of Death & Rebirth

The Eighth House dissolves the ego so something truer can be born.

Published: December 13, 2025 | Last Updated: December 13, 2025

What the 8th House Represents

After forming bonds in the Seventh House, the soul is tested through intimacy, loss, and surrender.

Astrological Principle:
The 8th House governs psychological depth, shared resources, power, and transformation.

This house rules death and rebirth (literal and symbolic), sexuality, trauma, inheritance, debts, taxes, and the occult.

Key Themes of the Eighth House

Area of Life Meaning
Transformation Endings, rebirth, psychological change
Intimacy Sexual bonding, emotional merging
Shared Resources Joint finances, inheritance, debts
Power & Control Trust, vulnerability, manipulation
Occult Astrology, psychology, taboo knowledge

Planets in the 8th House

Planets here intensify life experiences and force deep inner change.

  • Sun: Identity forged through crisis and rebirth
  • Moon: Emotional intensity, fear of loss
  • Mercury: Investigative mind, interest in taboo subjects
  • Venus: Passionate bonds, karmic relationships
  • Mars: Sexual intensity, power struggles
  • Saturn: Karmic debts, delayed transformation
  • Pluto: Profound regeneration, shadow mastery

Spiritual Lesson of the 8th House

The Eighth House teaches the soul that true power comes from surrender.

When integrated, this house grants wisdom, resilience, and the ability to guide others through darkness. When resisted, it manifests as fear, obsession, or control issues.
